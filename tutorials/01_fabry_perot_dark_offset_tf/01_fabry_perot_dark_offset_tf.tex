\textbf{Sensing the Fabry-Perot's Resonance}\\
Suppose we have a Fabry-Perot interferometer.
We would like to know whether the Fabry-Perot is on resonance.
Here we will explore possibilities for sensing the resonance of a Fabry-Perot cavity.

\end{align}
